About us

Waterproofing Masterssp is a market leader in the field of building preservation, here is some information about us and some of the benefits of using Waterproofing Masterssp Ltd:

Waterproofing Masterssp have over 10 years of experience of supplying structural waterproofing, torchon waterproofing, roofing, roofing repairs, repair of damaged gutters and painting services to both domestic and commercial properties throughout the Western Cape. Our offices based in Buh-Rein Estate, Cape Town puts us in the heart of Western Cape and allows us to easily offer our services throughout the region and to all areas in Cape Town. Our experience covers all aspects of structural waterproofing and includes cavity drain membrane,dry & wet rot, epoxy resin repairs, rising damp, TAM injections, waterproofing and woodworm treatment
